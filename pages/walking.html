Walking Tours

Free walking tours

Put on your walking shoes and be ready to explore the city! =)

I would like to welcome everyone to my free walking tours in St. Petersburg. Give me 2 hours and I will give you the heart of St. Petersburg!

Actually, there are no time limits. Walks vary in length as I usually take into account the wishes and suggestions of a group. I guarantee you will have enough time to “stay and stare”! There is no task to cover all places within strictly defined time boundaries! It is you who set the time and the pace!

It does not matter what the weather is in the city or how many people want to participate in a tour, I will walk with you through the history of St. Petersburg with a great pleasure!

I believe that free walking tour is a perfect introduction to St. Petersburg history, legends, and stories. I offer you to learn more about well-known places of St. Petersburg like the Sate Hermitage Museum or St. Michael’s Castle as well as to discover little-known streets and places.

Why should I choose free walking tours?

  1. You will see the major landmarks of St. Petersburg, so, the next day you won’t be lost in the city: you will have the idea where you are and what direction you should choose;
  2. You can receive more information about must-visit places, places to eat, places to shop and things that you can do during your stay;
  3. You can easily meet new people and enjoy a good company;
  4. It is the cheapest way to see St. Petersburg. There is no charge. At the end of a tour you can leave tips, but it is optional. Every traveler should enjoy the city and it does not matter what his or her budget is.

When?

You chose the time and day

Language?

English

Where does the tour start and end?

The meeting point depends on what tour you would like to choose (see list below). After the tour we can return to the meeting point or I can explain you what else you can see in the district.

How much walking is involved?

Once again it depends on you. We can visit several places for 2 or 4 hours or we can stay in front of a building, discussing its architectural peculiarities and history. You set the pace.

I can guide you anywhere in St. Petersburg. I am head over heels in love with it! There are some options:

  • City Center, meeting point Palace Square
  • Religious St. Petersburg or the City of Tolerance, meeting point Kazan Cathedral
  • Vasilyevsky Island, meeting point Palace Square
  • Blood Triangle, meeting point Church of the Savior on Blood
  • St. Petersburg, the Literary City
  • If you have any preferences, just let me know and I will design a tour specially for you!

How can I participate in a tour?

Tours are available only on request. Fill the form below and do not forget to indicate day and time when you would like to discover the wonders of St. Petersburg.
