Self-guided Walking Tours

Вс 27 Апрель 2014

I have created this section in hope that some of you will find it useful. Here I am not going to describe the well-known places of St. Petersburg as I am sure you will find a lot of information about them in the Internet. Half the fun of exploring the city lies in visiting “secret spots” that most of the tourists do not know about.

Best Hidden Spots for Cinema Lovers

Watch old films in the English language with subtitles. Do not forget to take a free of charge cup of tea.

  • http://vk.com/bez_popkorna

Best Hidden Spots for Café Lovers

Anti-cafes

Pay for time and not for tea. Free of charge tea, cookies, wi-fi and board games.

  • http://nevsky81.ziferblat.net/en/
  • http://vk.com/miracleclub
  • http://vk.com/12rooms

Best Hidden Spots for Museum Lovers

If you are eager to see Russia with its small villages and big cities, do not miss the opportunity to visit Grand Maket.

  • http://visit-petersburg.ru/?page=place_full_view&id=442&lang=en
  • http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hLxnIeDn7Nk

Best Hidden Spots for Walk Lovers

Mini St. Petersburg is hidden in one of the central parks of the city.

  • http://visit-petersburg.ru/?page=place_full_view&id=287
